A2 Go!

A2 Go! is an augmented reality mobile game set in Ann Arbor, Michigan. Ann Arbor is nicknamed "Tree Town", and in this game users can plant and grow different kinds of trees native to the area, fight the squirrels that frequent the Univerity of Michigan's campus, and unlock new kinds of seeds and the ability to buy a train ticket while learning about notable landmarks and exploring the city.

Technologies Used:
  • Unity 2022.3.44f1
  • JIRA
  • Mapbox
  • Mixamo
  • Sketchfab
  • Freesound
