CSE Simulator

CSE Simulator is a virtual reality recreation of BBB 1695: a computer lab in the Bob and Betty Beyster Building on the University of Michigan's North Campus. The computer lab has since been renovated, so this simulator serves as a historical record of what the room used to look like. In this simulator, players can interact with the world and experience a slice of what life was like for Computer Science students who worked and studied there.

Technologies Used:
  • Unreal Engine 5
  • Blueprints
  • Affordances
  • JIRA
  • Mixamo
  • Sketchfab
  • Freesound
